# Pharmacy Inventory & Prescription Manager

## Product Summary
A web application for independent pharmacy owners to manage drug inventory, track expiry dates, and maintain accurate prescription records — preventing expired stock from ever reaching patients and ensuring every dispensing event is traceable and accurate.

## Primary Users
- **Pharmacy Owners**: Independent or small-chain pharmacists who manage stock, staffing, and compliance. Direct decision-makers who bear financial and regulatory consequences from inventory errors and prescription inaccuracies.

## Core Problem
Pharmacy owners lose thousands annually to expired drugs that are never flagged in time. Manual or fragmented tracking creates dangerous blind spots — drugs near expiry remain on the shelf, prescription records become inconsistent, and audits expose costly compliance gaps that threaten the business.

## Core Solution
A single operational tool that:
1. Tracks all drug inventory with real-time stock levels and low-stock alerts
2. Alerts owners to drugs approaching expiry — 30, 60, and 90 days out — before the date arrives
3. Maintains accurate, searchable prescription and dispensing records for every event
4. Generates compliance-ready audit reports on demand

## Key Features
- **Drug Inventory Management**: Add, update, and monitor stock levels by drug name, batch, supplier, and category. Low-stock threshold alerts prevent stockouts.
- **Expiry Date Tracking**: Log expiry dates per batch. Dashboard view of drugs expiring within 30, 60, and 90 days. Automated alerts before critical windows.
- **Prescription Records**: Log dispensed prescriptions with patient identifier, drug, dose, quantity, date, and dispensing pharmacist. Fully searchable and filterable.
- **Expiry Dashboard**: Visual overview of upcoming expirations sorted by urgency and drug category.
- **Reorder Management**: Auto-generate reorder lists based on low stock levels and near-expiry flags together.
- **Compliance Reports**: Printable summaries of inventory status and dispensing history for regulatory audits.

## Strategic Goals
1. **Prevent expired stock**: Eliminate financial write-offs and patient safety risks from expired drugs reaching dispensing.
2. **Ensure prescription accuracy**: Create a reliable, searchable record of every dispensing event — reducing errors and supporting full regulatory compliance.

## Brand & Tone
Professional, clear, and clinically trustworthy. Built for operators who take accountability seriously. No jargon — direct, functional, and focused on outcomes.

## Scope & Positioning
MVP operational tool for independent pharmacy owners. Not a consumer app; not a multi-hospital EHR. Focused on the day-to-day operational layer: stock on shelf, expiry on the clock, prescriptions on record.
