Why This Exists

⚠ The Problem

Expired drugs are invisible until they're dispensed — costing money, endangering patients, and triggering audits.

  • ● Drugs expire silently on the shelf, creating financial write-offs and patient safety risks that only surface during an audit or incident
  • ● Manual prescription logs are error-prone, inconsistent, and nearly impossible to search quickly during a compliance review
  • ● Inventory blind spots cause overstock of near-expiry items alongside stockouts of needed drugs — with no early warning system
✓ The Solution

One operational tool that gives pharmacy owners full visibility before problems happen — not after.

  • ● Automated expiry alerts flag at-risk batches 30, 60, and 90 days out — every drug is checked before it can slip past the date
  • ● Digital prescription records capture every dispensing event accurately — searchable by patient, drug, or date in seconds
  • ● Real-time inventory dashboard shows stock levels and reorder needs at a glance — no spreadsheets, no guesswork
