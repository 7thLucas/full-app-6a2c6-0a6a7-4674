Core Features

💊 Pharmacy Owner
📦

Drug Inventory Management

Track stock levels by drug, batch, and supplier with low-stock threshold alerts to prevent stockouts.

⏱️

Expiry Date Tracking

Log expiry dates per batch. Automated alerts at 30, 60, and 90-day windows before any drug expires.

📋

Prescription Records

Log every dispensed prescription with patient, drug, dose, quantity, and pharmacist — fully searchable.

📊

Expiry Dashboard

Visual overview of upcoming expirations sorted by urgency and drug category — always up to date.

🔄

Reorder Management

Auto-generate reorder lists combining low-stock alerts and near-expiry flags into one action list.

📄

Compliance Reports

Printable summaries of inventory status and dispensing history ready for any regulatory audit.
